Online Supplement

The Online Supplement includes additional documentation for assessing and better understanding the Republican Baths at Pompeii: photos and a Structure from Motion Model of the Republican Baths, and photos and plans of Pompeian buildings cited as comparisons. The documentation is provided by members of the team and published here »su concessione del Ministero della Cultura – Museo Archeologicao Nazionale di Napoli« (Suppl. Fig. 1) and »su concessione del Ministero della Cultura – Parco Archeologico di Pompei« (Suppl. Figs. 2–55).
